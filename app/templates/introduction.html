Library Aid

A smart, scalable, and user-friendly personal library management system that combines traditional library principles with AI technology. It aims to empower users to efficiently organize, explore, and interact with their personal libraries, ultimately fostering a more productive and insightful research experience.

Features

AI-Powered Research

Utilize AI technology for Academic Research, and get personalized recommendations based on your interests. It also helps in creating AI abstract

Easily access academic resources

Store and access academic resources from anywhere, anytime, and on any device being it audio, pdf and videos.

Personalized Recommendations

Get personalized recommendations based on your interests and preferences. It helps in finding the right resources for your research.
